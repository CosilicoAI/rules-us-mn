# Minnesota Rules and Statutes

Minnesota rules and statutes in Akoma Ntoso XML format for machine-readable legal analysis.

## Structure

```
rules-us-mn/
├── statutes/           # Minnesota Statutes
│   ├── ch290-297/      # Taxation chapters
│   └── ch256/          # Human Services
└── regulations/        # Minnesota Rules (administrative regulations)
```

## Coverage

### Statutes

- **Chapter 256** - Human Services (public assistance, welfare programs)
- **Chapter 290** - Individual Income Tax
- **Chapter 290A** - Property Tax Refund
- **Chapter 291** - Estate Tax
- **Chapter 292** - Inheritance Tax (repealed, historical)
- **Chapter 293** - Gross Earnings Tax (repealed, historical)
- **Chapter 294** - Taxes on Petroleum and Tobacco
- **Chapter 295** - MinnesotaCare Tax
- **Chapter 296A** - Motor Fuel Taxes
- **Chapter 297** - Gasoline and Special Fuel Taxes
- **Chapter 297A** - Sales and Use Tax
- **Chapter 297B** - Motor Vehicle Sales Tax

### Regulations

Minnesota Rules (administrative regulations) implementing the above statutes.

## Source

- [Minnesota Statutes](https://www.revisor.mn.gov/statutes/) - Office of the Revisor of Statutes
- [Minnesota Rules](https://www.revisor.mn.gov/rules/) - Office of the Revisor of Statutes

## Format

All documents are encoded in [Akoma Ntoso](http://www.akomantoso.org/) XML format, an OASIS standard for legal documents.
